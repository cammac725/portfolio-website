What I've been doing with my time

I've done many things throughout my career. I've taught martial arts, built houses, and been an art framer. I've driven forklifts and construction equipment of all sizes. I was a Honda technician for over 20 years.

For the last 10 years, I have owned rock Tech Computer Services, Inc., first in the Seattle area, then on the North Olympic Peninsula. I serviced and supported my clients' computer needs, from building custom computers, removing malware, solving network, software, and security issues. I worked remotely, in shop, or on site.
